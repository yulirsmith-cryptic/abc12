Our Mission

Equity in perinatal health for every family

At Around-Birth Collective, we work to improve perinatal health in the Inland Empire and Inland North County San Diego — providing direct services, increasing diversity in the perinatal workforce, and advocating for equitable access to quality care.

We believe that every birthing person deserves to be seen, supported, and empowered — regardless of background, identity, or zip code.

"Ensuring the period 'Around-Birth' is safe and supported for every family."
