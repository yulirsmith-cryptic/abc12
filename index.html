About PHLA

Real policy power, community-led.

The Perinatal Health Leadership Academy is a 10-module, community-driven leadership training facilitated by Around-Birth Collective and our co-leads. It trains consumers and advocates of midwifery care to develop and advance state-level policy.

This cohort focuses on the legislative track — advancing California bills that expand access to midwifery care and community birth, from Medi-Cal supplemental payments for midwife-attended community births to fixing a malpractice insurance market that's collapsed to a single carrier. You'll learn how policy gets made, build advocacy and storytelling skills, and help move real legislation forward. No prior policy or advocacy experience required. We'll teach you everything.

The program runs August 2026 through August 2027. Most of our work happens virtually, with two in-person convenings during the year.

In a recent community survey, 100% of respondents believed PHLA can improve birth outcomes — with zero neutral or opposed. This is a program our community asked for.

Who belongs here

This is for you.

There's no single "right" background. PHLA is for consumers and advocates of midwifery care — parents, doulas, midwives, health workers, and organizers who have received or championed community birth.

i. A consumer or advocate of midwifery care
ii. Rooted in a California community Living, working, or organizing in California
iii. Ready to lead No prior policy experience needed
